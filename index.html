🏦
Your own digital safe. Unlike a bank or an app like PayPal, a self-custody wallet means only you hold the key to your funds — no company can freeze, access, or lose them on your behalf.
🔑
The private key = the key to the safe. It's a long secret code. Whoever has it controls the wallet. In Crescendo, this key lives on your device — not on our servers.
⛓️
Every stamp is a real record. When you approve a practice session, a small transaction is written permanently to the blockchain — a public, tamper-proof ledger. It's like a digital certificate that can never be faked or deleted.
⚗️
This is a test. The wallet address and transactions shown here are on Base Sepolia — a test network. No real money or crypto is involved. This is just so you can experience what it feels like!
